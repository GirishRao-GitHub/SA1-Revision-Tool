Every SA1 question maps to at least one pillar. Most questions draw on 2–3 pillars simultaneously — the examiner wraps a scenario around a core pillar and layers hooks from adjacent pillars. The India overlay (P6) is rarely a standalone question; it surfaces as hooks within P1–P5. PMI and IP products dominate the scenario pool but the pillars tested vary each sitting.
