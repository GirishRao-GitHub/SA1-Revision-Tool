Valuation basis: All assets at market (fair) value; technical provisions = best estimate liability (BEL) + risk margin (cost-of-capital approach, 6% CoC rate). BEL is a probability-weighted, risk-free discounted cashflow of all future in/outflows to policyholders. Intangibles (goodwill, DAC, internally-generated) are valued at nil. Deferred tax is recognised on all SII vs GAAP differences. Own funds = total assets − total liabilities on SII basis.

Regulatory templates: QRT S.02.01 (balance sheet); S.12.01 (Life & Health SLT TPs); S.17.01 (Health NSLT TPs); S.23.01 (own funds). SLT = "similar to life techniques"; NSLT = non-similar to life.
