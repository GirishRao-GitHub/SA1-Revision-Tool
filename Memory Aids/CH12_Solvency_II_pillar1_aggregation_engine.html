Key outputs

SCR = BSCR + SCR_op + Adj. The BSCR already reflects diversification between the five risk modules via the Annex IV correlation matrix. SCR_op is added without diversification credit. Adj is the loss absorbing capacity of technical provisions and deferred taxes (typically negative, reducing the SCR).

Tier summary

SCR: at least 50% must be Tier 1; Tier 3 must be less than 15% of SCR. MCR: at least 80% must be Tier 1; no Tier 3 permitted. Basic own funds = Tier 1 + Tier 2 + Tier 3. Ancillary own funds require prior supervisory approval and are not included here.
