MCR — linear formula + cap / floor
Key outputs
SCR = BSCR + SCRop + Adj. The BSCR reflects diversification via the 5×5 Annex IV matrix. SCRop is added without diversification. Adj is the loss-absorbing capacity of TPs and deferred taxes (typically negative). The reconciliation reserve is derived as Assets − TP − Sub liabilities − other equity items, ensuring the balance sheet closes exactly.
Tier summary
SCR: ≥50% T1; T3 <15%. MCR: ≥80% T1; no T3. Ancillary own funds (T2 ancillary) require prior supervisory approval and are included in total own funds but are off-balance-sheet. The reconciliation reserve is the residual of the SII balance sheet equation.
