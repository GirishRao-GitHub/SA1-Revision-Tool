Key outputs
SCR = BSCR + SCRop + Adj. The BSCR reflects diversification via the Annex IV 5×5 correlation matrix. SCRop is added without diversification credit. Adj is the loss-absorbing capacity of technical provisions and deferred taxes (typically negative, reducing the SCR). The health module SCR itself reflects three layers of diversification: sub-module within SLT, sub-module within Non-SLT, then SLT/Non-SLT/CAT via the 3×3 matrix.
Tier summary
SCR: at least 50% must be Tier 1; Tier 3 must be <15% of SCR. MCR: at least 80% must be Tier 1; no Tier 3 permitted. Basic own funds = Tier 1 + Tier 2 + Tier 3. Ancillary own funds require prior supervisory approval and are not included here.
