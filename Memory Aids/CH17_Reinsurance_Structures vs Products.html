⚠ KEY EXAM INSIGHT — Non-Proportional Structures and Long-Term Products:
For individual long-term products (IP, CI, LTCI), Risk XL, Aggregate XL and Cat XL are generally NOT APPLICABLE. The reason is structural: the sum insured is fixed and known at inception, so individual claim amounts cannot exceed the sum insured — meaning XL attachment is impossible. Proportional structures (QS and Surplus) are the primary tools. For group schemes, however, non-proportional structures are fully relevant because correlated claim events create genuine aggregate and catastrophe exposure.
